About Me

I am a New York-based publishing professional with over seven years of editorial experience. I have worked as an acquisitions editor at Farrar Straus Giroux Books for Young Readers, where I acquired and edited picture books, middle grade, and young adult projects.

Currently, I am a production editor at Scholastic, working on books for very young readers, as well as a freelance editor.

While I specialize in children’s books and YA, I have experience working with projects ranging from contemporary women's fiction to personal memoir.
