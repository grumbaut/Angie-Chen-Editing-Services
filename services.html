Services

Manuscript Evaluation

Congratulations! You’ve just completed your first draft. Now you need someone to read through and isolate any areas that need more unpacking and further development.

Are you having trouble figuring out what you want to say? I can ask you the right questions to get at the heart of your story.​ ​Or perhaps you have written several drafts and now you just need an honest, unbiased perspective.

Book this service if you have not been through any revisions nor have you had any professional readers. ​​A manuscript evaluation includes an editorial letter that covers all of the broad-scope issues in your manuscript.

Developmental Editing

This service is a little more involved than a manuscript evaluation. In addition to focusing on the broader editorial issues, I will also provide narrower-in-scope, specific comments within the text.

Do you feel like something is missing in your story, but you’re not quite sure what exactly? I can work with you to figure it out. I will read through your manuscript and focus on content, structure, and style. Does your overall story work? Is the narrative arc satisfying? Is the internal structure of each chapter consistent throughout the manuscript? Is a character coming across as too caricatured? Does your protagonist need more motivation? Does some of the dialogue feel forced? I will offer suggestions, comments, and edits, but I’ll be careful to maintain your authorial voice.

Line Editing

After you have nailed down your foundation and the broader aspects of your manuscript, you will be ready for more in-depth editing​. ​​​I will go through sentence by sentence and offer suggestions/edits on flow, structure, voice, and other micro-level concerns.​

​This service is essentially editing that does not focus on the broader editorial concerns that are covered in the developmental editing service.

Query Letter Review

​Your manuscript has been through countless laborious revisions and now you are finally ready to pitch to your favorite agent. As a former acquisitions editor at a major publisher, I have reviewed many pitches from both agents and authors, and I have a strong sense of what works and what doesn’t work in a query letter.

I can help you hone and sharpen your pitch to make it as compelling as your story. I can offer you guidance on creating a query letter that not only sells your story, but more importantly, piques the agent’s interest. A well-written query can elevate your submission above the others in the slush pile, and I can help you take it there.

Synopsis Editing

​​​​An essential component to your initial pitch, a synopsis can make or break your query. ​This service is usually booked together with query letter editing. ​I’ll go through sentence by sentence and ensure that each word you use should be there.​ I will advise on how you can shorten, lengthen, and/or refine your synopsis.

Copy Editing

​​​​If your manuscript ​has been through several revisions and you have resolved all creative content issues, this service is for you. Unlike developmental editing or line editing, copy editing focuses on grammar, spelling, punctuation, and syntax. I will also ensure that you are maintaining internal consistency with your dialogue, characters, setting, etc.

Your manuscript should be in its final form before you consider a copy editing service.
